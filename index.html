TripHop Mobile App

An app for all your trip planning solutions!

The Problem

Travellers these days do a lot of research while planning their itinerary. They have a hard time collating ratings and reviews over various websites/apps.

The Solution

To provide an app where users can see all the required information at a glance for their ease in comparison.

User Insight

Karan, who loves to travel with his family, needs to have an easy way to find a reasonable place to stay, good food, and travel excursions without having to do his own exhaustive time-consuming research, because of, the lack of up to date websites and good information he is frustrated with his planning results. During the interviews and by the result of the survey, we discovered that the travelers get frustrated while researching to build up their own itinerary. Therefore, we believe that planning the entire trip, which includes a reasonable place to stay, exquisite food and places to move around is quite cumbersome and that we might be able to help if we [could provide for a platform where people can cross compare and get their bookings done. We might do this by providing at a glance comparison for hotels and a list of highly rated and reviewed eating joints along with popular things to do. Doing this will allow our product to [offer better planning resources for the users to successfully plan their itinerary.

Problem Statement

We have observed that people find it easier to plan their vacation when they can compare the ratings and reviews of travel activities, comfortable accommodation and authentic food. However, there are currently too many websites and resources to review, which makes the research process lengthy and creates a lot of stress in planning a trip. How might we help travellers compare the ratings and reviews of various destinations to reduce travel planning stress and optimize their trip with the highest rated activities?
